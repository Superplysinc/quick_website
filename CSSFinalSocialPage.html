Social Life

Attending a school like Phillips Academy is very stressful and rigorous. All throughout my Andover career, I have been told by numerous people that Andover is a place you cannot possibly go through by just yourself. Especially with it being upper year, I cannot agree with it more. Luckily, I have a great group of friends at my side that have been at my side and are there whenever I am in need of support or just a good time in the common room after a long day of school and extracurriculars such as swimming and cello. After Andover, the best memories are not the things that I had learned in the classrooms but rather the small things such as hanging out in the great lawn, or a goofy 3 AM late night Tetris gaming session.
