Swimming

Having swam my entire life, I am extremely passionate about the sport of swimming. It is quite ironic given that I had almost drowned my swim instructor as a baby due to my fear of putting my face in the water. Now, I have developed to be quite the swimmer having won an individual New England title for my age group, and being a core member of the well established Andover Boys Swimming Varsity team. My favorite events are the 100 yd Butterfly, 200 yd Butterfly, 200 yd Freestyle, and 100 yd Freestyle. I am currently looking at athletic recruitment, and hope to eventually swim at the NCAA Division 1 Championships
